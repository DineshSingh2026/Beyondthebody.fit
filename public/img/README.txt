REQUIRED: Copy your logo files into THIS folder with these EXACT names:

  btb-logo.png        — Beyond The Body logo
  bodybank-logo.png   — Body Bank logo

Full path on your computer should be:
  ...\btb\public\img\btb-logo.png
  ...\btb\public\img\bodybank-logo.png

The website loads them from /img/btb-logo.png and /img/bodybank-logo.png.
If you use .svg instead, rename to btb-logo.svg and bodybank-logo.svg, then update index.html (search for ".png" in the img src attributes).
